Kinde builds beautifully simple dev-acceleration infrastructure for SaaS teams. We provide an easy-to-use platform and code support for building:

- [Authentication and access](/authenticate/about-auth/about-authentication/)
- [Environment management](/build/environments/environments/)
- [Feature flags](/releases/about/about-feature-flags/)
- [User management](/manage-users/about/)
- [B2B/B2B2C infrastructure](/build/organizations/add-and-manage-organizations/)
- [Webhooks](/integrate/webhooks/about-webhooks/) and [connections](/integrate/connected-apps/add-connected-apps/)
- Custom [data](/properties/work-with-properties/manage-properties/), [tokens](/properties/work-with-properties/properties-in-tokens/), [brand](/design/brand/global-brand-defaults/), and [domains](/build/domains/pointing-your-domain/)
- [Customer billing](/billing/about-billing/about-billing/)

Supported by [SDKs](/developer-tools/about/our-sdks/) in more than 20 different language frameworks, your engineers can focus on building your core product, without also having to build the essential functions that allow your business scale and grow.

## What you can do with Kinde

### Authentication and access

Add the following to your SaaS app:

- Email/Username authentication
- Password authentication
- Passwordless OTP authentication
- Phone, SMS, WhatsApp auth
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Social logins (Google, Facebook, etc.)
- Enterprise SSO (Microsoft Entra ID, SAML, etc.)

Without writing any auth logic yourself. Give your users a seamless, secure sign-in experience from day one. [Learn more about authentication and access.](/authenticate/about-auth/about-authentication/)

### Environment management

Keep development, staging, and production cleanly separated so your team can ship changes without touching live data. Manage configs per environment and move faster with fewer production incidents. [Learn more about environment management.](/build/environments/environments/)

### Feature flags

Roll out new functionality to specific users or segments without redeploying your app. Run controlled releases, A/B tests, and kill-switch risky changes in real time. [Learn more about feature flags.](/releases/about/about-feature-flags/)

### User management

Search, view, and manage every user across your platform from a single dashboard. Update roles, suspend accounts, and act on user data without needing direct database access. [Learn more about user management.](/manage-users/about/)

### B2B/B2B2C infrastructure

Model your customers as organizations with their own users, roles, and settings right out of the box. Give enterprise clients the multi-tenant experience they expect — without the months of custom build time. [Learn more about organizations.](/build/organizations/add-and-manage-organizations/)

### Webhooks and third-party integrations

Trigger automated workflows the moment key events happen in Kinde, and connect to the third-party tools your stack depends on without custom middleware. [Learn more about webhooks](/integrate/webhooks/about-webhooks/) and [connected apps](/integrate/connected-apps/add-connected-apps/).

### Custom branding

Change Kinde to match your:

- Custom domain
- Email sender
- SMS sender
- Brand colors
- Custom design

Extend users and organizations with your own properties, inject custom claims into tokens, and apply your brand across every customer touchpoint. Serve your product on your own domain for a fully white-labeled experience. [Learn more about custom data](/properties/work-with-properties/manage-properties/), [tokens](/properties/work-with-properties/properties-in-tokens/), [brand](/design/brand/global-brand-defaults/), and [domains](/build/domains/pointing-your-domain/).

### Customer billing

Bill your customers with Stripe. Attach subscription plans and entitlements to users and organizations directly in Kinde. Simplify how you manage access tiers without bolting on a separate billing layer. [Learn more about customer billing.](/billing/about-billing/about-billing/)

### 20+ SDKs and frameworks supported

Kinde supports 20+ SDKs and frameworks, including:

- [Next.js](/developer-tools/sdks/backend/nextjs-sdk/)
- [React](/developer-tools/sdks/frontend/react-sdk/) ([React Native](/developer-tools/sdks/native/react-native-sdk/), [Tanstack Start](/developer-tools/sdks/backend/tsr-sdk/))
- [Flutter](/developer-tools/sdks/native/flutter-sdk/)
- [.NET](/developer-tools/sdks/backend/dotnet-sdk/)
- and many more.

Drop Kinde into the stack you already use — no rewrites, no lock-in. You can also [use Kinde without an SDK](/developer-tools/about/using-kinde-without-an-sdk/). [Explore all SDKs.](/developer-tools/about/our-sdks/)
