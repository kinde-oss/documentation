To support the delivery of our services, Kinde may engage and use data processors (each, a “sub-processor“) with possible access to external user personal data that is uploaded, accessed, processed, or transmitted to and from our services. This page provides important information about the identity, location, and role of each sub-processor.

Notifications of changes will be provided to customers who have signed a Data Processing Agreement (DPA).

The sub-processors used will depend on the features and integrations in use.

External users are customers of Kinde’s customers.

## Sub-Processors - Authentication

Kinde will share external user personal information to the following third parties to facilitate the authentication service.

Note: Location is aligned with the customer’s selected Kinde region.

| Service provider | Entity type          | Link                                              | Location                                          |
| ---------------- | -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| AWS              | Public cloud hosting | [https://aws.amazon.com](https://aws.amazon.com/) | Australia, Ireland, United Kingdom, United States |

## Sub-Processors - Subscription billing

Kinde will share external user personal information to the following third parties to facilitate the subscription billing feature.

Note: Location is aligned with the customer’s selected Kinde region with the exception of Stripe. Due to the nature of Stripe’s services, all data is processed through the United States.

| Service provider | Entity type           | Link                                              | Location                                          |
| ---------------- | --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| AWS              | Public cloud hosting  | [https://aws.amazon.com](https://aws.amazon.com/) | Australia, Ireland, United Kingdom, United States |
| Stripe           | Billing and invoicing | [https://stripe.com](https://stripe.com/)         | United States                                     |
