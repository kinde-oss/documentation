<Aside type="upgrade">

You need a [paid plan](https://kinde.com/pricing/) to remove Kinde branding ("Powered by Kinde") from your authentication pages.

</Aside>

Kinde authentication pages (sign-in, sign-up, OTP page, self-serve portal, etc.) include "Powered by Kinde" branding by default.

<img src="https://imagedelivery.net/skPPZTHzSlcslvHjesZQcQ/fc430432-4ccb-4c62-cbbf-13b3783aa000/socialsharingimage" alt="Kinde authentication page showing Powered by Kinde attribution" width="400" />

To remove Kinde branding:

1. In Kinde, go to **Settings > Business > Details**.
2. Scroll to the **Global display settings** section.
3. Turn off **Show Kinde branding**.

    ![Show Kinde branding switch](https://imagedelivery.net/skPPZTHzSlcslvHjesZQcQ/48f9983e-01f5-4aef-7c3a-41c5958eeb00/socialsharingimage)
4. Select **Save**.

The change takes effect immediately for all your applications. You do not need to redeploy.
