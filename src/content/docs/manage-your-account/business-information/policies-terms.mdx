Add **Terms of use** and **Privacy policy** URLs to your business in Kinde.

## Add policy URLs

1. Go to your Kinde dashboard, select **your business name** in the bottom-left corner. A pop-up appears.

2. In the pop-up, select **Business details**.

    ![Business details popup](https://imagedelivery.net/skPPZTHzSlcslvHjesZQcQ/562db191-bb01-47e4-48b6-640c99ac6a00/socialsharingimage)

3. In the **Terms of use and privacy** section, enter the publicly available URLs for your:

    - Terms of use URL
    - Privacy policy URL

4. Select **Save**.

    After you add your Terms of use and Privacy policy URLs, users will see these links on the **Sign up page** in the widget footer.

    ![Terms of use and privacy section](https://imagedelivery.net/skPPZTHzSlcslvHjesZQcQ/eace6e01-6501-4375-3fb9-29a14c50a900/socialsharingimage)

    <Aside>
        When your users sign up or sign in to your business via the Kinde platform, they implicitly agree to your **Privacy policy** and **Terms of use** (or licence terms).
    </Aside>

## Switch on explicit acceptance of policies

<Aside type="upgrade">
You will need a paid plan to use this feature. Check out the [Kinde pricing page](https://kinde.com/pricing/) for details.
</Aside>

Some businesses want to record explicit acceptance and track who has accepted. This is also known as a clickwrap agreement, click-accept, click-to-sign, or clickthrough agreement.

To switch on explicit acceptance, follow these steps:

1. Go to **Global display settings** section.
2. Switch on the **Show a policy acceptance checkbox on sign up** toggle.
3. Select **Save**.

<Aside>
When you switch on explicit acceptance, it is set at the business level and will apply to all environments and organizations in your business.
</Aside>

### Acceptance experience for users

The next time a user signs up, they are prompted to select the acceptance checkbox before they can sign up for your service.

- If they sign up with email, the acceptance checkbox appears inline on the sign-up page (see image above).
- If they sign up using a third-party service like Google or GitHub, a dialog appears, prompting them to accept (see image below).

### Check if a user has accepted

You can see in a user’s profile when they accepted the policies, terms, and conditions.

1. In your Kinde dashboard, go to **Users**.
2. Filter the list and select the user you want to view.
3. Select **Activity**.
  
  The **Terms of use accepted** and **Privacy policy accepted** dates appear in the description. You will also see them in the **Log** list. These appear as log activity because when a policy is updated and new acceptance is recorded, the accepted date also changes.
